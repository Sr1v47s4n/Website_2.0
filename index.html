About Us


"Energy Club: Fuelling Tomorrow's Visionaries!"
Step into the vibrant world of the Energy Club at Easwari Engineering College! We're a dynamic club; we're a movement fueled by the spirit of sustainability and powered by the passion to protect our planet. Through captivating webinars, enlightening seminars, and impactful campaigns, we're igniting minds to champion sustainable energy practices.
Dive into a five-day extravaganza of technology and sustainability at our flagship events, Negizhi and Technotsav. It's the playground where innovation harmoniously combines eco-consciousness, and where dreams of a greener future come alive. Join us as we flip the switch to a more sustainable tomorrow, one idea at a time.
Unleash your energy and curiosity with the Energy Club, where every spark fuels a brighter, cleaner world. Embark on a journey that celebrates technology, fosters awareness, and empowers the next generation to build a planet-friendly future. Together, let's light up a path towards a greener horizon!
