Negizhi 4.0

The Energy Club of Easwari Engineering College is an interdepartmental technical club which has been conducting multiple events to instill the knowledge of Energy Sustainability and Conservation. We, the TITANS of this tenure have taken up the initiative to organise NEGIZHI 4.0, the flagship event based on emphasizing the importance of "PLASTIC AWARENESS". The main motto of this event is to inculcate knowledge about the harmful and perilous effects of plastic which jeopardize our environment on every day basis. The impact of plasticity has been cultivated in the pupil's mind through a variety of exciting events. Neghizi focuses on educating the student society about how non biodegradable materials like plastic accumulate and cause, severe life threatening hazards such as intoxication, land pollution, water pollution, soil corrosion, etc. This is the very high time, our club has extended hands in educating the public through various awareness raising events. Let's join our hands together to make a better future by making NEGIZHI 4.0 a grand success through our scintillating events.
